About Us

Phymatics Champions Club is an academic club that brings students together to discuss ideas and concepts in Mathematics and Physics. Discussions are led by the general discussant. And contributions or participations are open to the members of the club present.

  • Aim at academic improvement
  • Aiming at collaboration and cooperation
  • Improving on our communication skills
  • Improving our creativity and problem solving skills

Phymatics champions club welcomes everyone to join at no cost. Certificates are issued out every year for the best participants and to the out going carbinet for the great services provided

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Rational For The Establishment

    • Why was phymatics champions club started?

      Tr. Bazira Dathan decided to start up this descusion club due the difficulty that existed toward students understanding physics and mathematics concepts and saw it of importance if students could gather as a club and have some time to go through the challenging concepts in the above two subjects.It was started so as to equip learners or students with more problem solving skills in both physics and mathematics.

    • How is the club organisation Management?

      The club is led by a patron at the top and a carbinet of 20 learders that is led by a club president. other carbinet members include a general discussant, mobilizers,speaker,treasurer, among others.

    • What is the main Objective of phymatics champions club?

      Our main objective is to improve on the performance and problem solving skills, creativity and innovation.

    • What does one gain from joining this club?.

      There are plenty of advantanges for joining this club;

        Improvement of performance.
        Improvement in creativity and innovation.
        One gains exposure and social connection from fellow students and discusants from different classes.
        Certificates of membership are issued.
    • What does it cost to join this club?

      Joining Phymatics Champions Club is free and cost nothing for anyone willing to join this club.
