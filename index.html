About Us

Discover the history and purpose behind the Phymatics Champions Club.

Phymatics Champions Club is an academic club that brings students together to discuss ideas and concepts in Mathematics and Physics. Discussions are led by the general discussant. Contributions and participations are fully open to all members of the club present.

  • Aiming at continuous academic improvement
  • Fostering collaboration and cooperation
  • Improving our critical communication skills
  • Enhancing creativity and problem-solving abilities

Phymatics Champions Club welcomes everyone to join at no cost. We believe knowledge should be accessible to all who seek it.

Outstanding participants and the outgoing cabinet are awarded certificates annually for their dedicated services and active involvement in shaping brilliant minds.

Frequently Asked Questions

Rational Details for The Establishment

  • Why was Phymatics Champions Club started?

    Tr. Bazira Dathan decided to start this discussion club due to the widespread difficulty students faced in understanding physics and mathematics concepts. He saw immense value in gathering students to collaboratively tackle challenging concepts, equipping them with advanced problem-solving skills.

  • How is the club organization managed?

    The club is led by a Patron at the top, followed by a cabinet of 20 leaders headed by a club President. Other cabinet members include a general discussant, mobilizers, speaker, and treasurer, among others.

  • What is the main objective?

    Our main objective is to dramatically improve academic performance, critical problem-solving skills, and to foster creativity and technical innovation.

  • What does one gain from joining this club?

    Benefits of joining include:

    • Significant improvement of academic performance.
    • Boosts in creativity and innovation.
    • Exposure and social connection with fellow students across different classes.
    • Official Certificates of Membership upon completion.
  • What does it cost to join this club?

    Joining Phymatics Champions Club is completely free and costs nothing for anyone willing and motivated to learn.
